SHORT SUMMARY ABOUT ME

Hi, I am André and a more or less experienced software developer / system administrator and much more. Currently I am attending the HTL Villach for Information Technology with the main focus on network technology, so I could already gain some experience in the technical area. Also privately I am very interested in the field of server administration and therefore I could already gain some experience with different technologies.
